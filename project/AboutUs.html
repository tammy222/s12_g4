About Us

Recipe Explorer is a recipe-sharing website developed as part of our university web programming project. As a team of passionate students, we wanted to create a site that not only showcases our coding skills but also highlights the incredible variety of dishes from around the world. Our website is a place where you can explore and try out delicious recipes from all corners of the world, whether you're an experienced cook or just starting your journey.

How It All Started

This project is for our web programming course, where we were tasked with building a website using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We chose to focus on international recipes because we believe that food is a universal way to connect people. Through Recipe Explorer , we hope to inspire you to experiment with new dishes and discover exciting flavors from various cultures.
